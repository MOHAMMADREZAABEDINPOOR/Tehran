Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about bookings and events

How far in advance should I make a reservation?

We recommend making reservations at least 1-2 weeks in advance for regular shows, and 2-4 weeks for special events or large groups. Popular events may require even more advance notice.

Can I cancel or modify my reservation?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your reservation up to 24 hours before the event. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to make changes.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! Please let us know about any dietary restrictions or allergies when making your reservation, and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs.

Is there a dress code for events?

Most events have a casual dress code, but some special events may have specific requirements. We'll inform you of any dress code requirements when you make your reservation.

Can I book Tehran for private events?

Yes! Tehran is available for private events, corporate functions, weddings, and other special occasions. Please contact our bookings team for more information and pricing.
